Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ current_date }}

NamTix ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ticketing platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, payment details when you purchase tickets
  • Event Information: Events you attend, purchase history, preferences
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device characteristics
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process ticket purchases and manage your account
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Send event recommendations and promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Event Organizers: Necessary details for event access and management
  • Payment Processors: To complete transactions (PayGate Namibia, MTC Money, etc.)
  • Service Providers: Companies that help us operate our business
  • Legal Authorities: When required by Namibian law

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure storage of payment information
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

5. Your Rights

Under Namibian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your account data (subject to legal requirements)

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze site traffic
  • Improve user experience

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or platform announcements.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries:

Email: privacy@namtix.com
Postal: Data Protection Officer, NamTix, Roentgen Street, Windhoek, Namibia
