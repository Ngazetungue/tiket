Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NamTicket

Purchasing tickets is simple! Just browse our events page, select the event you want to attend, choose your ticket type and quantity, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payments.

We accept multiple payment methods including:

  • Visa, Mastercard, and other major credit/debit cards
  • Mobile payment options (MTC, Telecom, etc.)
  • Bank transfers (for certain events)
  • PayPal for international attendees

After completing your purchase:

  1. You'll receive an email confirmation with your tickets attached as PDF
  2. Tickets will also be available in your NamTicket account
  3. For mobile tickets, you can access them through our mobile app

Our refund policy depends on the event organizer's preferences. Generally:

  • Most events offer refunds up to 7 days before the event
  • Some special events may have non-refundable tickets
  • If an event is canceled, you'll automatically receive a full refund

To create an event and sell tickets:

  1. Click "Create Event" at the top of the page
  2. Fill in your event details (name, date, location, etc.)
  3. Set up your ticket types and pricing
  4. Configure any additional options (seating, discounts, etc.)
  5. Publish your event and start selling tickets!
