About NamTix

Namibia's premier ticketing platform for seamless event experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2023, NamTix was born out of a passion for connecting people with unforgettable experiences. We recognized the need for a reliable, Namibia-focused ticketing platform that caters specifically to our unique cultural events and entertainment landscape.

What started as a small Windhoek-based startup has now grown into the nation's most trusted ticketing solution, serving event organizers and attendees across all 14 regions.

Our Mission

To revolutionize event access in Namibia by providing a seamless, secure, and user-friendly ticketing platform that empowers organizers and delights attendees.

We're committed to fostering Namibia's vibrant entertainment industry while making event discovery and attendance effortless for everyone.

Why Choose NamTix?

Secure Payments

Mobile-Friendly
